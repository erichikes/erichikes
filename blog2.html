A Beautiful Brown Trout

It was June of 2019, I was fresh off graduating from my undergraduate college program and ready to take on the world. When I say I was fresh off graduation, I mean that I had graduated the day before and was currently in route to the mountains of Wyoming with 3 other friends. I had done a little backpacking and camping before, but really could be considered a novice; though the manly side of me thought I was ready for anything that could come my way....including a grizzly bear.



We were set to make the trek back into the wilderness of the Tetons for a 5 night backpack and camping trip. The picture you saw on the homepage of this website was likely the first picture I snapped as I stood in awe of the lake and mountain view at hand. Our good Lord had really worked his artistic touch all over the location we would be spending the week at.



The week was moving right along and we were trying our hand at fishing for the lake trout each day we could. My friends had caught a couple smaller brook trout, but we weren't having amazing success at fishing. I decided to take a shot at fishing on a day we had planned to lounge around camp and take it easy. With my trusty yellow and red Jake's spin-a-lure tied up and ready to go, I headed down to the lakeside.

As I casted away and tried my hand at fishing, I was beginning to think that I would strike out again. On the next cast I made, I felt some resistance on my line, but still got my lure all the way in. Was I just having fantom bites or was something playing with my lure? I casted again and to my suprise something decent nailed my lure! Game on! I began to fight the fish I had on the line and wear it down. It certainly fought to get lose, but I kept at it and brought it closer and closer to the bank. Relieved that I had the trout just where I wanted it, I pulled the fish out of the water to see a nice sized brown trout! Actually...this was the biggest one any of us had caught all week!



Excited about the catch, I called out to my friends and they all came around for pictures and to enjoy the moment. This was my first ever trout and it did not disappoint. There has never been anything quite like catching a lake trout from fresh mountain lake water since this moment! A beautiful lake brown trout and a memory to last forever!
