2024 Green River Lakes WY Gear Dump

When it comes to taking a trip into the backcountry, preparation and preparedness are key. The gear you pack and how you pack it can make or break your trip's success.

In 2024, a group of 7, including myself, made the trek to the Upper Green River Lake and Squaretop Mountain located in the Wind River Range of Wyoming. The trip included backpacking from the trailhead to our desired basecamp overlooking the lake and beautiful view of Squaretop Mountain, camping 4 nights in the wilderness at our basecamp, taking day hikes in the area, and hours of fishing for lake trout.

As I packed for this backpacking, camping, and fishing trip; I knew that it was a tall task to plan for a group of our size. I had never taken a trip of this caliber with this many people at once. I started with my trusty google sheet of items needed for a trip of this nature. It had guided me in preparation for trips before, so I knew it could be trusted.

As I checked off items from the checklist, I had a clearer path on what we needed as a group or that I needed to consider purchasing. The following is a list of my packed items. You can also watch my youtube video on this gear dump here: Coming Soon
